What is Anko?

あんこ（Anko）is basically a sugar-sweetened paste of mashed red-beans.
Through out the history, Anko has been a crucial element of the Japanese confectionary scene.
For the confectionary served in luxurious tea-ceremony to sweets that kids could by in food stand, you could see a number of ways to enjoy it.

Let's see how Anko is enjoyed in Japan!
